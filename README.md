# GardenRewards - Community Garden Management and Harvest Rewards System

A blockchain-based community garden management and harvest rewards system built on Stacks, incentivizing sustainable urban agriculture through transparent cultivation tracking and fair reward distribution.

## Overview

GardenRewards enables community gardeners to track their cultivation activities and earn harvest rewards based on their gardening contributions, promoting local food production and sustainable agriculture practices.

## Features

- Garden activity tracking with cultivation effort verification
- Harvest reward calculation based on cultivation levels
- Seed preservation system with time-based incentives
- Cultivation level progression and seasonal bonuses
- Community garden capacity management

## Smart Contract Functions

### Public Functions
- `start-garden-activity`: Begin garden cultivation activity
- `complete-garden-harvest`: Complete harvest and earn rewards
- `claim-harvest-rewards`: Claim accumulated harvest tokens
- `preserve-seeds`: Preserve seeds for future planting
- `release-preserved-seeds`: Release preserved seeds with bonuses

### Read-Only Functions
- `get-garden-activity-count`: Get gardener's total activities
- `get-harvest-token-balance`: Get gardener's harvest token balance
- `get-cultivation-level`: Get gardener's cultivation level
- `get-garden-stats`: Get overall garden statistics

## Usage

Start garden activities, complete harvests to earn rewards, and preserve seeds for enhanced future yields. Build cultivation levels through consistent gardening participation.

## Security

- Garden supervisor oversight and capacity management
- Time-based validation for cultivation activities
- Early harvest penalties to encourage proper preservation
- Cultivation effort verification and reward calculation
